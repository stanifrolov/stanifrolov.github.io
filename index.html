Stanislav Frolov

Hi, I'm a 3rd year Ph.D. student at TU Kaiserslautern and German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence, advised by Prof. Andreas Dengel. During summer 2021, I interned at Adobe Research working with Kushal Kafle on image editing.

My main interests are computer vision and machine learning. I use deep generative models for controllable image synthesis and representation learning.

Previously, I've worked at inovex as a data engineer, and completed my B.Sc. and M.Sc degrees in Electrical Engineering at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology.

Research

I'm interested in image synthesis, and particularly, controlled image synthesis. There are multiple ways to gain control over the image generation process such as conditioning or learning disentangled representations. I'm also interested in automatic evaluation techniques for vision & language systems which is a challenging problem.

If you are interested in similar topics, want to chat or collaborate, feel free to contact me.
